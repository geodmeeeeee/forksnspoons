### Game Details & Guide
Game Overview


Rabbit Samurai is a browser game that combines elements of strategy, exploration, and combat to create an engaging experience. The game takes place in feudal Japan, where players control a rabbit character as they navigate through a vast world filled with challenging enemies, hidden treasures, and mysterious landscapes.


How to Play


To play Rabbit Samurai, you will use your mouse to control the rabbit character's movements and actions. The objective of the game is to defeat enemy rabbits while collecting coins and avoiding deadly traps.

The gameplay consists of exploring the world, fighting enemies, and collecting resources. You can move the rabbit character by clicking on it, jump by pressing the space bar, and attack enemies by clicking on them.


Controls and Objective

• Move the rabbit character left or right using the mouse wheel
• Jump by pressing the space bar
• Attack enemies by clicking on them
• Collect coins by defeating enemy rabbits

The objective of the game is to defeat all enemy rabbits in each level before time runs out.


Tips and Tricks for Success

• Explore the world thoroughly to find hidden treasures and secret paths
• Use your space bar to jump over obstacles and avoid danger
• Attack enemies at close range using the mouse click on their heads
• Defeat enemy rabbits in a single hit to earn bonus rewards
