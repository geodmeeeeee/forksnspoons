Game Overview


Choppy Orc is a puzzle game developed by CoolmathGames, where players take on the role of an orc who must chop down trees in a forest while navigating obstacles and collecting power-ups. The goal is to reach the end of each level while avoiding falling logs, rolling boulders, and other hazards.


How to Play

• Move your orc by pressing the corresponding arrow keys or WASD keys on your keyboard.
• Chopping down trees will decrease your health points. If you run out of health points, the game is over.
• Your goal is to reach the end of each level while avoiding obstacles and collecting power-ups.
• Power-ups include increased speed, extra life, and temporary invincibility.
• You can also collect coins by chopping down trees. Coins can be used to purchase upgrades for your orc.


Controls

• Arrow keys or WASD keys: Move your orc
• Space bar: Chop down a tree
• C and V keys: Increase and decrease speed respectively
